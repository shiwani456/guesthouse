About

Banasthali Vidyapith has its own Guesthouse inside the campus. The Guesthouse has various types of rooms that include Delux Rooms, Super Delux Rooms, Superior Rooms, Delux Dormitory Rooms, Dormitory Rooms.


It also provides Mess(food) services which is optional for the visitor. There are certain guidelines that are strictly followed for allocating rooms to the visitors.


These are:
ROOM:-

  • 1. Each room can be allocated to a maximum of 2 people.
  • 2. The charges of the respective rooms are to be paid on a daily basis for all the types of rooms except Dormitory. For Dormitory, charges are to be paid per bed daily.

NOTE:-

Room number 501,502,601 and 602 are reserved for the guests of Banasthali Vidyapith.
